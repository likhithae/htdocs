MooMa is an agricultural, diary milk technology venture (AgTech) improving diary milk farm efficiency and productivity, by empowering diary farmers and enterprises.

MooMa’s diary solutions uses technology to help farmers keep their cows healthier and more productive, creating new economic opportunities that transform communities.

“Happy cows - Happy farmers”

MooMa empowers Diary Milk farmers by improving farm efficiency, milk yields and managing larger herds of livestock sustainably.
